Week 11: Apr 4, 6, 8

Objectives: Understanding Argument for Project 3, Part 2

Due Monday 4/4

Homework

  • Project 3, Part 1 is due on Canvas or via email by 5pm

Readings

  • None

Due Wednesday 4/6

Homework

  • Continue your research for Project 3, Part 2; begin thinking through your stakeholder for the issue and the thesis statement you'd like to offer to guide your researched argument for that audience.

Readings

  • None.

Due Friday 4/8

Homework

  • Write a draft of your thesis statement for P3:P2.
  • Bring your draft to class for a mini-workshop.
  • Sign up for next week's conferences.
  • Review the Conference Preparation Worksheet; consider starting on this over the weekend in preparation for conferences next week.

Readings

  • JTC Ch. 10 pp. 380–382 and 402–410 (stop at "Ensuring Integrity"; we'll read this section next week)
  • JTC Ch. 14 pp. 500–504 about Thesis Statements
