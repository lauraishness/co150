Weekly homework assignments and readings.


Week 15: Dec 5, 7, and 9

Objectives: Style & Conventions for R & C / Workshop P4 / Course Evaluations

Due Monday 12/5

Homework

  • Begin drafting / revising your resume & cover letter, making note of the rhetorical choices you're making in writing them.

Readings

  • Optional: Resumes & Cover Letters Writing for the Technical Professions Link available soon.

Due Wednesday 12/7 -- Workshop Resumes & Cover Letters

Homework

  • In-class workshop for resumes & cover letters. Bring a hard copy of at least 80% of both of these AND submit on Canvas
  • Also bring with you a list of 5-10 rhetorical decisions/choices that you plan to discuss in your rhetorical analysis. This is a simple, bulleted list.
  • OptionalBring a draft of your rhetorical analysis

Readings

  • None.

Due Friday 12/9 -- Mandatory attendance for Course Evaluations

Homework

  • Continue working on P4.
  • Make sure you come to class prepared to complete our course evaluations. This is an important part of getting your voice heard with the University on how coursework decisions get made.

Readings

  • Review career center resources for resumes / cover letters: Career Center Website

Due Thursday 12/15 -- In place of Final Exam

P4 / Final Exam

  • Review the assignment sheet for P4 and be sure to complete all components and turn in via Canvas or email by 5pm on Thursday 12/15
    • Part 1:
      • Resume
      • Cover Letter
      • Link or text from the job posting
    • Part 2:
      • Rhetorical analysis (700-1000 words)
  • Make sure you come to class prepared to complete our course evaluations. This is an important part of getting your voice heard with the University on how coursework decisions get made.
